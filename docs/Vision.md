# Vision

## Product
CarrierGuard AI

## Mission
Help freight brokers verify carriers in under 30 seconds using AI-powered compliance and risk analysis.

## Vision
Become the easiest and most trusted carrier vetting platform for small and medium-sized freight brokerages.

## Problem

Freight brokers manually verify carriers by checking multiple websites, insurance records, and safety data.

This process is:
- Slow
- Repetitive
- Error-prone
- Difficult to audit
- Creates legal risk

## Solution

CarrierGuard AI automatically:

- Retrieves carrier information
- Verifies authority
- Checks insurance
- Calculates risk
- Generates AI explanations
- Creates compliance reports
- Saves audit history

## Target Customers

- Freight Brokers
- Operations Managers
- Brokerage Owners

Company Size:
1–20 employees

## North Star Metric

Average carrier verification completed in under 30 seconds.

## Success Metrics

- First 10 paying customers
- 95% successful searches
- <5 second search response
- 80% monthly retention

## Long-term Vision

Become the operating system for carrier compliance and risk management.
